## Mobile vs Desktop Experience

### Mobile (Priority)
- Vertical stacking
- Touch-friendly buttons (min 44px)
- Circular avatars scale down
- Timeline bars stack vertically
- Modals full-screen on small devices

### Desktop
- Max width 600px for readability
- Centered content
- Side-by-side layouts (before/after)
- Timeline horizontal bars
- Modals overlay with dimmed background

## A/B Testing Opportunities

1. **Social Proof Numbers**
   - Test: "5,245+ People" vs "Thousands of People"
   - Test: Show/hide star rating

2. **Commitment Modals**
   - Test: 2 modals vs 3 modals (like Spartan)
   - Test: Yes/No vs single Continue button

3. **Timeline Duration**
   - Test: 12 weeks vs 8 weeks vs "Results in days"
   - Test: Bar chart vs line graph

4. **"Why We Ask" Placement**
   - Test: Below questions vs above questions
   - Test: All 3 vs remove 1-2

5. **Motivational Page**
   - Test: Before/after images vs text only
   - Test: 3 benefits vs 5 benefits
